NCASD Alumna Lorien Jasny Promoted to Senior Lecturer

We are very pleased to announce that NCASD alumna and CNRA fellow Lorien Jasny has been promoted to the position of Senior Lecturer in the Department of Politics at the University of Exeter.  This promotion, the equivalent of promotion to Associate Professor in the American academic system, recognizes Dr. Jasny's significant body of contributions to social networks, public policy, and the impact of the structure of beliefs on behavior.  A major theme of Dr. Jasny's research is the relationship of individuals' cognitive structures to the environment in which they reside, a critical factor in problems such as conflict resolution and policy implementation.  Congratulations to Dr. Jasny on this achievement!
