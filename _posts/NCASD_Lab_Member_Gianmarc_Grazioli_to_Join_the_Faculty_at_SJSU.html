NCASD Lab Member Gianmarc Grazioli to Join the Faculty at SJSU

Congratulations to NCASD lab member Gianmarc Grazioli, who has accepted an Assistant Professor position in the chemistry faculty at San Jose State University.  In addition to allowing him to continue his pathbreaking work in computational chemistry, the SJSU position will give Dr. Grazioli an opportunity to further develop his interests in chemical education (as exemplified by his well-known "Shredded Science" YouTube series, and his other instructional projects).  As part of the NCASD lab, Dr. Grazioli has been a key player in advancing the group's work on machine learning-based analyses of high-dimensional trajectory data, and on the development of novel coarse-graining techniques for the modeling of amyloid fibril formation.  Congratulations to Dr. Grazioli on this next big step in his career!
