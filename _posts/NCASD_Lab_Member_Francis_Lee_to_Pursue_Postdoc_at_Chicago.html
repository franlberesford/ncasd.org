NCASD Lab Member Francis Lee to Pursue Postdoc at Chicago

Congratulations to NCASD lab member and recent graduate Francis Lee, who will departing this spring to pursue a postdoctoral fellowship position with the Schneider lab at the University of Chicago.  In his new position, Dr. Lee will apply his expertise on network sampling and statistical network modeling to the study of vulnerable populations coping with health challenges such as HIV and opiate addiction.  In his graduate work at the NCASD lab, Dr. Lee made important contributions to the study of network measurement from error-prone informant reports and to the modeling of social phenomena such as stigma and ostracism in social networks.  Congratulations to Dr. Lee as he embarks on this next step in his career!
